Important Instructions

Instructions
1Read all the instructions carefully. No further query in this regard will be entertained.
2The leave should be applied at least 24 hours before the leave time. The warden will approve the leave every day between 6-8 PM (approximately). No phone call from the main gate will be entertained.
3You have to upload a duly signed consent form at the time of applying for leave. If you have to attend any social function at your home, plan your travel accordingly. No further leave will be granted once you are back from this one-time post mid sem break.
4The form you are submitting is only meant for the "leave management". For make-up or any other academic-related queries, please contact your respective course instructor(s)/Academic Divison.
5All the actions after pressing the "submission" button are irreversible. You can not change the dates of leaving and returning to the campus. Be careful while submitting.
6Download the PDF of the approved leave form on your mobile which you can show at the main gate.
7In the reason box, please be specific about the purpose of leave for example going home for festival or going for examination. Personal reasons etc kind of arguments may lead to the rejection of the application.


For any query related to Leave Application, Please Contact to Student Welfare Division (SWD)
